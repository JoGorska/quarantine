How to Play the Game

SMASH! POW! CRUNCH! A brick wall appears at the top of the screen, and your missions is to smash all walls off the playfield -- one brick at a time.

Paddle

Players on pc can use mouse to control the movement of the paddle, while mobile phone users can use their finges to slide across the screen.

Start of the game

Game loads with the ball attached to the paddle. If the user clicks with the mouse, the ball is released and the game starts. The mobile phone users can use the screen touch instead.
