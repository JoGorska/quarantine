How To Play The Game

SMASH! POW! CRUNCH! A brick wall appears at the top of the screen!
Your mission is to smash all walls off the playfield - one brick at a time.

Paddle

Players on PC can use the mouse to control the movement of the paddle, while mobile phone users can use their fingers to slide across the screen.

Start of the game

The game loads with the ball attached to the paddle. Click the mouse (PC) or touch the screen (mobile) to release the ball and start the game!

Winning The Game

Hit the blocks with your ball to destroy them. When all the blocks are destroyed, you win the game!

If the ball goes past the paddle, off the bottom edge of the screen then you will lose the game
